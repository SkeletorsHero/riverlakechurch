In whatever you’re going through right now you need to know that your feelings will not get you to where you need to be.  They never will.  The constantly fail you and you know that.  They fail you in your eating habits, your job expectations (how many wanted to sleep in today), etc.

If we did everything we wanted to (or felt like doing) then we would all be in a lot of trouble.  In fact some of your best moments are when you didn’t say what you wanted to that rude person who cut you off or that boss who blamed you for their mistakes.  Our feeling constantly and consistently fail us.  

BUT, more importantly they also reveal where we have been faithful.  I don’t mean faithful like you think I do.  I mean faithful as in what we have consistently done over and over again to the point that our hearts and minds are drawn to that activity.  

Illustration of runners and the runners high…feeling (the high) comes later…and in some cases…much later.
