GOD



35 By this time it was late in the day, so his disciples came to him. “This is a remote place,” they said, “and it’s already very late. 36 Send the people away so that they can go to the surrounding countryside and villages and buy themselves something to eat.”

  • What was said, verbally or non verbally was ‘We’re hungry!'
  • What was heard was really different
    • The disciples heard…there is a problem…they should fix it.
    • Jesus heard…there is potential to help…we should fix it!
  • They didn’t want to serve but to direct.  I find that the church is full of people who want to direct but not serve.
    • (I can sit here for a second)
  • Step 1 - Don’t listen?
    • Do you hear your kids
    • Do you hear your spouse
    • Do you hear your boss
    • Do you hear your body
    • Do you hear your budget?

37 But he answered, “You give them something to eat.”

  • How would you respond to that command?
  • Jesus puts it right back on them for them to fix.
  • If you feel/think/see it you need to fix it.
  • See they are in harvest season and the disciple think…we’ve arrived…look at all these people.  

They said to him, “That would take more than half a year’s wages! Are we to go and spend that much on bread and give it to them to eat?”

  • I used to look at this as the didn’t have it...
  • I don’t think that is the issue...It’s a waste of money…it wasn’t they didn’t have it…it’s they didn’t want to spend it.
  • It was greed…so many times in our lives we are so focused on wasting money we forget it really isn’t ours to begin with.


38 “How many loaves do you have?” he asked. “Go and see.”
  • To often we reject ideas before we evaluate what we even have.
  • They don’t even know what they have.
  • Do you know what you have?  
  • I have found that many have more than they know they do
    • You need to do a spiritual, relational inventory of where you are.
    • Some people talk themselves out of what God has them up for.  (Could be good here)
  • What are you’re talking yourself out of that God is trying to talking you into?
  • Because for some of you today you will discover you have more than you realize.
    • More potential
    • More friends
    • More energy
    • More left in the tank
    • More...
  • But until you take inventory of where you are…you’ll never discover where you could go.

When they found out, they said, “Five—and two fish.”
  • What can Jesus do with this?  This is NOTHING
  • What they heard - that is nothing - Yanni
  • What Jesus heard - That’s enough - Laurel
  • 5 & 2…what was said…but what was heard is two different things.
  • God’s not looking for your harvest, he’s looking for your heart

39 Then Jesus directed them to have all the people sit down in groups on the green grass. 
40 So they sat down in groups of hundreds and fifties. 
  • We find out a little later that there was about 5000 men so probably close to 20,000 people maybe.
  • No matter how many there are thousands of people there..it’s a lot!!!


41 Taking the five loaves and the two fish and looking up to heaven, he gave thanks and broke the loaves. 
  • Jesus blessing the bread
  • Step 2 - Don’t bring it
  • Jesus won’t bless what you won’t bring
    • Some are wanting to reap a harvest from a field they have never sown into
    • (Sit here for a second if I have the time)

Then he gave them to his disciples to distribute to the people. He also divided the two fish among them all. 42 They all ate and were satisfied, 
43 and the disciples picked up twelve basketfuls of broken pieces of bread and fish. Mark 6


  • See the harvest was here…but what they heard determined what they did
  • The harvest is always the hardest but the blessing is worth the battle

WE

  • Gospel illustration with Laurel and Yanni - it’s not what is being said but what is being heard.
  • The reason we hear different things is because as we get older we lose sensitivity to high pitch sounds and so it distorts what we hear.
    • What is really being said is Yanni…not laurel…but you can’t convince of that…I just have to trust someone else.
    • Salvation and the Gospel is just like that.
    • What is being said is Jesus saves, He loves you, He came and died for you so that you could have a new and better life here on earth and in the after life.  
    • You don’t have to live the life you are currently living…you can be free.
  • But this isn’t what everyone hears is it?
    • Some people hear religion
    • Some people hear fairy tales
    • Some people hear judgement and condemnation
  • So what do you hear today?
