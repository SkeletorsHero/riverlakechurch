Differences between the three:

  • Pride
  • Sloth
    • Desire for REST
    • The Color LIGHT BLUE
    • Virtue: Diligence
  • Vainglory
    • Desire for ATTENTION
    • The Color PURPLE
    • Virtue: Humility
  • Wrath / Anger
    • Desire for HARM
    • The Color RED
    • Virtue: Patience
  • Envy
    • Desire for OTHERS SUCCESS
    • The Color GREEN
    • Virtue: Kindness
  • Gluttony -
    • Desire for EXCESS -
    • The Color ORANGE
    • Definition: The consumption of excess of anything and everything to fulfill a desire.
    • Scripture: 
    • Virtue: Temperance
    • Gluttony differs from Greed in that Gluttony resolves around a personal behavior.
    • Gluttony differs from Lust in that Gluttony focuses on "more".
  • Greed
    • Desire for THINGS -
    • The Color GOLD
    • It is the collection of something that is never to be released. You want all of whatever it is: Money, a Collection, Prizes, Etc...
    • Scripture: 
    • Virtue: Charity, Giving
    • Greed differs from Gluttony in that Greed revolves around a social behavior.
    • Greed differs from Lust in that Greed is selfish.
  • Lust
    • Desire for PLEASURE
    • The Color BLUE or PINK
    • The goal is self pleasure normally at the objectification of another.
    • Scripture: 
    • Virtue: Chastity - original definition didn't mean celibacy but monogamy (I could use this somehow)
    • Lust differs from Gluttony in that Lust focuses on pleasure.
    • Lust differs from Greed in that Lust is shareable.
