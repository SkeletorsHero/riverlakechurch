There are triggers in peoples lives…in fact that is sort of a buzz word right now.  Everyone is being triggered by something.  No matter how you feel about that there is a lot of truth to it though.  We all have triggers.  Not that send us in a tirade and we have to feel offended, but triggers that move us to patterns of behavior.

For all of us we know the problems but we continually find ourselves in places we don’t want to be…many times the same places we don’t want to be.  It’s the trigger.  It is the first initial actions that sets us on a course of action to fire a bullet and hit a target.  And unlike in the movies…I’m not sure it’s possible to catch the bullet once it’s fired.  

So the goal then becomes not to catch the bullet but to not pull the trigger.  The problem many of us have with this though?  The trigger isn’t bad!  The trigger isn’t wrong.  The trigger, is amoral.  Here are some common triggers...
