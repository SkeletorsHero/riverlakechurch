Who We Are

A Community,
Not Just a Network

Landmark isn't a franchise or a denomination. It's a family of like-minded pastors who are genuinely committed to one another — sharing life, sharpening each other, and holding each other accountable to God and the calling He placed on their lives.

When you join Landmark, you gain a tribe. People who will show up, help fund your vision, walk through the hard seasons with you, and celebrate your wins as their own. This is what it means to be part of something bigger than yourself.

Our Mission

Starting. Strengthening. Setting.

Three words that define everything we do as a network — and the posture we hold toward every church in our family.

01

Starting

We believe the world needs more churches, not fewer. Landmark actively supports new church plants with financial resources, coaching, and the backing of an established community from day one.

02

Strengthening

Existing churches grow stronger through regular coaching, One Day events, shared wisdom from peer pastors, and access to resources that help leaders and congregations thrive at every stage.

03

Setting

We set churches free to be fully themselves — planted in their community, rooted in the gospel, and empowered to multiply. We don't clone churches; we help them become what God designed them to be.

Iron Sharpens Iron

From regular gatherings and One Day coaching events to global mission trips that build deep bonds — we create the moments that make community real.
